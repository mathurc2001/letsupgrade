AERIAL YOGA CLASSES

Aerial Yoga is a hybrid exercise form comprising of traditional yoga poses, pilates and dance. It makes use of a silk hammock, suspended over a yoga mat. It adds an interesting challenge in completing complex asanas. It helps you burn body fat while building toned and lean muscles. Want to achieve a Zen state of mind? Then this one’s for you!

BENEFITS OF AERIAL YOGA

  • Strengthens core muscles
  • Increases blood flow and supply
  • Works in reducing migraines and improves breathing
  • Aids in improving posture
  • Helps increase spinal flexibility and movement
  • Calms you and reduces stress
